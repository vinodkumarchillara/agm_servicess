Ultrasonic Testing

Visual Testing

Surface-level inspection for cracks, corrosion, and more.

PMI Testing

Verifies metal composition using XRF or OES technology.

Liquid Penetrant Testing (LPT/PT)

Liquid Penetrant Testing (LPT/PT) is a non-destructive testing method used to detect surface-breaking defects in non-porous materials using a dye or fluorescent penetrant.

Magnetic Particle Testing (MPT/MT)

Magnetic Particle Testing (MPT/MT) is a non-destructive testing method used to detect surface and near-surface discontinuities in ferromagnetic materials by applying magnetic fields and ferrous particles.

Ultra Sonic Thickness Gauging (UTG)

Ultrasonic Thickness Gauging (UTG) is a non-destructive testing method that uses high-frequency sound waves to measure the thickness of materials from one side, commonly used to assess corrosion or erosion.

Tensile Testing

Measures the strength and ductility of materials under tension.

DT Services

Destructive Testing (DT) services in mechanical testing involve methods like tensile, impact, bend, and hardness tests to evaluate the mechanical properties and structural integrity of materials by subjecting them to conditions that lead to failure

Hardness Testing

Hardness Testing is a destructive or semi-destructive method used to measure a material's resistance to deformation, typically by indentation, using scales like Brinell, Rockwell, or Vickers.

Bend Testing

Bend Testing is a destructive mechanical test used to evaluate a material’s ductility, flexibility, and resistance to cracking by bending it to a specified angle or until failure.

Proof Load Testing

Proof Load Testing is a type of mechanical test where a structure or component is subjected to a specified load—usually higher than its normal working load—to verify its strength and structural integrity without causing permanent deformation or failure.

Charpy Impact Testing

Charpy Impact Testing is a destructive test method used to measure the amount of energy absorbed by a material during fracture at high velocity, indicating its toughness and resistance to sudden impact.

AAS

Atomic Absorption Spectroscopy (AAS) is an analytical technique used to determine the concentration of specific metal elements in a sample by measuring the absorption of light by free atoms.

Spark Spectro

Spark Spectrochemical Analysis is a technique used to determine the elemental composition of metallic samples by exciting the surface with an electrical spark and analyzing the emitted light spectrum.
